STICKER STAR KIDS


I designed this simple high fidelity prototype using FIGMA. It's my design for a digital reward application for itinerant specialists and service providers working in elementary schools. Speech-Language Pathologists, Occupational Therapists, APE Specialists and other non-classroom educators and specialists often travel to multiple locations and don't have dedicated space, time or money for traditional reward systems. STICKER STAR KIDS is the perfect solution!
